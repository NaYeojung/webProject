Gyeongpo Provincial Park

It is a place where natural monuments such as swans and mallard ducks come to visit. Gyeongpodae, one of the eight scenic spots in the Gwandong region, is famous as a place to view the full moon, and fishing at Gyeongpo Lake is enough to relax those who visit this place.

Jumunri Village

Jumunri Village is the most central village in Jumunjin-eup. It is the place where the commercial district is formed and the population is the most concentrated, and is currently made up of 12 administrative districts.

Anbandegi Village

A mountain is a cabbage field, and a cabbage field is a mountain. Because the slope is so steep that mechanical farming is impossible, this is a place that was created entirely through the efforts of farmers.

Noam Tunnel

Noam Tunnel is a hidden attraction in Gangneung that starts from Ipam-dong and extends to Wolhwa Street in Jungang Market. It used to be a road where trains actually came and went, but with the construction of the KTX railway, it lost its original function and was transformed into a tourist attraction.
