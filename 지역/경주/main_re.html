Lee Pungnyeo Guro Ssambap

Ipungnyeo Gurossambap, located near Daereungwon in Gyeongju-si, is known as a Korean restaurant that serves so many side dishes that your legs will break. The menu includes ssambap and tteokgalbi, which can only be ordered additionally.

Banwolseong Wood-fired Pizza

Banwolseong wood-fired pizza, located in Gyeongju-si, Gyeongbuk, is famous for its light and chewy taste as it is kneaded according to the Italian method and the aged dough is baked directly in an oak wood-fired oven at a high temperature of 400 degrees.

[Baeknyeon Store] Sookyeong Restaurant

The president, which is bent in a half-moon shape, is famous nationwide, and around the beach, there are not only restaurants that create a unique atmosphere, but also Haejangguk Alley and a distinctive cafe street.

millstone soft tofu

Millstone Soft Tofu, located at the entrance of Bomun Tourist Complex in Gyeongju, is always crowded with tourists visiting Gyeongju. Millstone Soft Tofu’s menu includes milled soft tofu, soft tofu stew, whole pork barbecue, and mung bean pancakes made daily with domestic beans.

Dolphin Sashimi Restaurant

Dolphin Sashimi Restaurant on the Sea is located close to Najeong Port. You can see the sea off Gampo from inside the restaurant. The representative menu is the luxury Gampo Mulhoe.
