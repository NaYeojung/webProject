Flavius

Cafe Flavius ​​is a cafe located in the Bomun Tourist Complex in Gyeongju. It is popular for its exterior that makes you feel like you are at the Colosseum in Rome, Italy.

Yellow donut

Yellow Donut is located in Hwangnam-dong, Gyeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do. The modern exterior and interior creating a clean atmosphere stand out.

Marche

Cafe Marche is a Hanok cafe located in Hwangnam-dong, Gyeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do. The exterior looks old-fashioned, but once you go inside, the desserts are cutely displayed.

El Garden

El Garden is a cafe located in Hadong, Gyeongju-si, Gyeongsangbuk-do. The exterior, which stands out for its modern sensibility, and the interior, which creates a sophisticated atmosphere, stand out.

Hwangnam Bread

Gyeongju Hwangnam Bread was created in Hwangnam-dong, Gyeongju in 1939, and was called Hwangnam Bread by people who bought bread, which became its current official name.
