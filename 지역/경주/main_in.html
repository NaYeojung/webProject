The K Gyeongju Hotel Spa Hot Springs

The K Gyeongju Hotel Spa Hot Springs is a hotel located within the Bomun Tourist Complex in Gyeongju, the 1,000-year old town of Shilla. The hotel opened in June 2001, and Spa World has facilities such as a sauna, open-air bath, gym, and swimming pool.

Raseonjae Korean History, Culture and Food School

Raseonjae, the Korean History, Culture and Food School located in the Bomun Complex in Gyeongju, is a place where you can experience our traditional food culture and is equipped with facilities such as classrooms, a restaurant, a museum and a data room.

In the middle of Gyeongju

A hanbok rental store located in the middle of Hwangridan-gil (Poseok-ro). Hanbok is available in a variety of materials, colors, and seasons. There are many Hanbok styles that reflect the latest trends.

Gyeongju Safety Experience Center

Gyeongju Safety Experience Center, Gyeongsangbuk-do Office of Education, is a comprehensive safety experience center centered on experiential education built to help students recognize and prevent the risks of safety accidents in their daily lives and to develop response skills appropriate for each disaster situation.
