A scenic tour with a bicycle

  • 1

    Sangju Bicycle Museum

    In the bicycle city of Sangju, the nation's first Sangju Bicycle Museum opened at 229-1, Namjang-dong, Sangju-si on October 26, 2002. In order to contribute to the development of bicycle culture and revitalization of bicycle use in line with low-carbon green growth, it was expanded and relocated to 415 Yongma-ro, Sangju-si on October 27, 2010. did.

  • 2

    Donamseowon

    The term 'Taonan' originates from the words, "Our Tao will be practiced in the south in the future," when Jeong Jeong of the Northern Song Dynasty sent his disciple Yang Xi to his hometown. This academy was created out of pride in the fact that Joseon's tradition of Confucianism lies in Yeongnam. was born.

  • 3

    Cheongnyongsa Temple

    It is said to have been founded in 1674 (the 14th year of King Hyeonjong's reign), and records related to its reconstruction are said to be kept in the nearby Donamseowon (道南書院). The temple consists of the main hall, Geungnakjeon, Sansingak, Yosa, and Bell Pavilion.

Historic cultural
heritage in Yangyang

  • 1

    Osan Prehistoric Site

    It is a ruins located in Osan-ri, Sonyang-myeon, Yangyang-gun, Gangwon-do, and is one of the earliest Neolithic period ruins on the Korean Peninsula. The historic site is located on a sand dune near the lake called 'Ssangho', about 200m inland from the east coast.

  • 2

    Osan Beach

    Osan Beach, located about 6km northeast of Yangyang-eup, is a small beach with a white sand beach of 900m in length, 80m in width, and a depth of 1m. You can taste fresh seafood such as sea squirts and sea cucumbers on the spot, and sometimes even enjoy clam hunting.

  • 3

    Jeonginhoe Restaurant

    It is said that Naksan Jeonginhoe Restaurant mainly receives live fish from Geojin Port, Daejin Port, Namhae, and Jumunjin and uses them for no more than a day.

  • 4

    Naksansa Temple

    Naksansa Temple, located on the northern coast of Yangyang-gun, is a temple built by Master Uisang, a high priest of Silla, after receiving a revelation from Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva, with Obongsan Mountain in the background. One day, Uisang heard a rumor that Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva was staying in a seaside cave and came here to see him in person.

Standing on Dokdo from Ulleungdo

  • 1

    Dokdo Observatory Cable Car

    Dokdo Sunrise View Cable Car Sightseeing View of the Mysterious Island Ulleungdo from the Sky While taking the cable car up from Dodong Mineral Water Park to the top of Manghyangbong Peak, you can admire the squid fishing boat Eohwa, one of the Eight Scenic Views of Ulleungdo, and check the weather at the Dokdo Observatory. On a clear day, you can even see Dokdo. It is well known as a sunrise spot, and the Dokdo Museum is located next to the boarding area.

  • 2

    Dodong Lighthouse (Haengnam Lighthouse)

    Haengnam Village, where the lighthouse is located, is a village along the coast between Do-dong and Jeo-dong and is located in the easternmost part of Ulleungdo. It is a warm village where you can see apricot flowers even in winter, and it is said to have been known as Haengnam (杏南) because there was a large apricot tree at the entrance of the village.

  • 3

    Bongrae Falls

    The water here comes from precipitation that collects in the Nari Basin in the northwest and seeps underground, and the pressured water from the underground finally gushes out to the surface. A waterfall was formed when a large amount of water rose to the surface and flowed down along the undulations of the terrain. The daily flow rate is approximately 3,000 tons or more, and nearby there are windholes, tent houses, and forest bathing areas where wind blows at 4°C all year round.
